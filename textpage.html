what used to be here?



I returned to the area of my high school a few years after I graduated and found that every place I had written into my memory had disappeared; the Mike's Papaya, the too-expensive corner diner, the musty bookstore where I had kissed my first boyfriend. What they had been replaced by was not of note. This site is not an elegy nor a narrative of gentrification. It's a place to deposit memories, of what used to be.
